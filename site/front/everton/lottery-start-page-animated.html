Shop as normal and get amazing Everton products for free

Simply install the Everton app and shop as normal at any retailer. When you visit their site you will see the option to generate Everton credits at the same time. Our clever system then tracks your visit and once the retailer has confirmed a sale, we put the credit in your account. Normally 5% of whatever you spend. When you want to redeem it, just click through to the Everton store via our link and complete your purchase using a code we provide you.
