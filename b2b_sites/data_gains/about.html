About Us

Data Gains offers Football Clubs, Charities, Telcos and Media Organizations branded proprietary Mobile Browsers and PC browser extensions that allow their supporters to earn (at no cost to themselves and with no change of behaviour) from their online shopping, data, searching and general usage of the product.

Data Gains allows organisations to gain a more targeted understanding of their audience, through big data, whilst at the same time rewarding them.

With a simple one-click download, users can be supporting their charity or club, earning money and winning prizes without using losing any functionality of Safari or Chrome, nor any change of their search, shopping or browsing behaviour.

It is a proprietary, cloud hosted solution, utilising machine learning and social graph to reward and engage users with real intelligence.

Data Gains has 3 subsidiaries: Premier-Browser, Media-Browser, and Charity-Browser
