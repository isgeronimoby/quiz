Frequently asked questions

Do I have to install the Everton Browser to enter the challenge?
No. You just need to get one person to install to be in with a chance of winning.
Is there a limit to the number of friends I can invite?
No. The more people you invite and sign up the more chance you have of winning.
What does an ‘Active’ user mean?
An Active user is a friend who has installed the Everton Browser Extension, signed up and made a purchase through the browser, so that their account balance is 0.01p or above. This ensures the friends you sign up are real people.
How can I invite friends?
You can invite friends by email, using our email notification engine, via Facebook, Twitter or by copying and pasting your unique link anywhere you like. You can paste this link into SMS, emails or even webpages and other social media. Anyone who clicks on this link and signs up will be associated to you.
Why is my friend’s status ‘not active’?
Friends must install, sign up and make at least one purchase through the browser, so that their balance is 0.01p or above, to be counted as an ‘active friend’.
What if my friend is not an Everton fan?
If your friend is not an Everton fan they can still use the browser to earn credit when shopping. They can then redeem this credit for cash instead of credit at Everton Direct.
Does the Challenge or browser cost me anything?
No. The challenge is free to enter and the Everton Browser is free to use too. It never costs you anything extra when shopping.
When does the challenge end?
The challenge finishes on the 19th December. Friends will need to be active on this day in order for them to count towards your total.
When do I find out if I have won?
All prizes and winners will be announced on the 20th December.
