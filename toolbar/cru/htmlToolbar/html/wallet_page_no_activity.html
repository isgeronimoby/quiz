How does it all work?

All retailers have a commission built into the price of their online products for marketing. Wishgenie has partnered with 1000's of retailers who have agreed to pass this commission to a chosen charity. It never costs you anything extra as the donation is made by the retailer. By signing up you will be notified each time your shopping results in a donation to charity.
